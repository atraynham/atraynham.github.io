Studio Session

The Specs
Style: Pilsner
ABV: 4.5%
The Brief

Studio Session is an ode to the crunch before a big deadline. A celebration of excess screen time, creative block, and the understanding that your friends/family/roommates aren’t going to see you for a while. It’s got just enough of a buzz to keep the creative juices flowing all night long.

Safelight

The Specs
Style: Red Ale
ABV: 6.0%
The Brief

Illuminate your night with the amber glow of Safelight. Dedicated to the mystery and anticipation of the darkroom. Dodge your negatives and toast your positives. Take a sip and see what develops.

India Ink

The Specs
Style: IPA
ABV: 7.2%
The Brief

Elegant, exotic, aggressive, crisp, and bold. India Ink represents precision and technique. Not a wise choice of medium for the timid or unskilled. Always drink with a steady hand.
