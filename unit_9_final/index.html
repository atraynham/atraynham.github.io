Our Story

Riley Brew Co. began with a creative spark, as they say. A flash of inspiration among fellow art students while spending long studio hours together at the Riley Hall of Art & Design. Our community of innovators and creators (and occasional imbibers, and possibly also procrastinators) decided to dabble in a new art form. And what better way to celebrate the sleepless nights of college (both inside and outside the studio) than with a pint of our own expression? We raise our glass to Riley.

Our Portfolio

Studio Session

The Brief

Studio Session is an ode to the crunch before a big deadline. A celebration of excess screen time, creative block, and all-nighters. It’s got just enough of a buzz to keep the creative juices flowing.

The Specs

4.5% ABV, Pilsner, Bright & Refreshing

Safelight

The Brief

Illuminate your night with the amber glow of Safelight. Dedicated to the mystery and anticipation of the darkroom. Dodge your negatives and toast your positives. Take a sip and see what develops.

The Specs

6.0% ABV, Red Ale, Malty & Toasty

India Ink

The Brief

Elegant, exotic, aggressive, crisp, and bold. India Ink represents precision and technique. Not a wise choice of medium for the timid or unskilled. Always drink with a steady hand.

The Specs

6.5% ABV, IPA, Herbal, Bitter & Citrus
